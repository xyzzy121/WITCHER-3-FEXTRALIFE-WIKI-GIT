            Blacksmithing

            Blacksmithing in the Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, is the art of repairing, creating and improving one's Weapons. This can only be done by trained Blacksmiths in the game. Not all Blacksmiths are created equal and you will need the very best to make the best gear. Please see the Blacksmiths page for their locations. Diagrams are needed to create Weapons from scratch. These can be found or purchased throughout the game.

            On every Diagram the craftsman's level requirement is displayed. You will need a craftsman of equal or higher level of expertise to craft specific items.

            There are 3 craftsman levels: "Amateur", "Journeyman" and "Master". Amateur and Journeyman levels are releativily easy to find.

            Only a select few are Master level and you must complete their questline to gain their services.

            A video guide of how to use a craftsman's services can be found .
