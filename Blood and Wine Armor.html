            Equipment and Crafting / Armor
              +

            Blood and Wine Armor in the Blood and Wine DLC expansion for The Witcher 3 is listed here. Detailed locations on where to acquire them are located on their individual pages.

            The Grandmaster Craftsman is a new merchant introduced in the expansion and is the only individual capable of upgrading gear to grandmaster quality. He is located in Toussaint's capitol, Beauclair, in the town's southern portion. Speaking to him will launch quests that will guide you to the diagrams for the grandmaster gear.

            Blood and Wine Armor Set Bonuses


            Several of the armor sets have bonuses when partially or fully complete along with their counterpart Weapons and they are as follows:

            Set
            		 3/6 Pieces
            		 6/6 Pieces
            Manticore Set
            		 Achieve critical hits with bombs.
            		 Additional charge for each alchemy item.
            Grandmaster Wolven Set
            		 Apply up to 3 oils to a sword at once.
            		 Throw bombs without delay.
            Grandmaster Ursine Set
            		 Chance to spawn a Quen shield for free after the one cast shatters.
            		 Increases the damage of Quen related abilities by 200%.
            Grandmaster Feline Set
            		 10% more fast attack damage for 5 seconds after a strong attack for each piece.
            		 Rear attacks do 50% damage and stun enemies.
            Grandmaster Griffin Set
            		 Cast a sign for free for 3 seconds after casting a sign using stamina.
            		 Yrden has +40% trap size and increased stamina regen and sign intensity while in it as well as damage reduction.
            Tesham Mutna Set
            		 Killing opponents restores vitality at a rate of 1% of your max vitality for every piece worn, to a max of 6%.
