            Bombs are Items in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. They are projectiles that can be thrown at opponents to cause various effects. Geralt can improve his Bombs by putting Skill Points into the Bomb Creation Skill Line. Below is a list of all the Bombs in the game:

            Bombs

            Damage: 100
            Duration: 4 secs

            2x Sulfur
            • Harpy Feeding Ground in Velen, Herbalist's Hut in Novigrad
            • Oxenfurt- The Manuscript for this bomb can be bought from the Blacksmith in Oxenfurt
            • Sund-  Within a chest, on an island north of Sund would be another copy of the manuscript.
