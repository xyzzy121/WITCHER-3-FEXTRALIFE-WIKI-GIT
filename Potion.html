            Potions in the Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, are Items that Geralt can use to temporarily buff his stats or grant certain effects, such as the ability to see in the dark, vitality regeneration, etc. Geralt can have two such Potions equipped at any given time to use on the fly. The following is a list of Potions in the Witcher 3: Wild Hunt and the ingredients needed to make them

            (Please keep in mind that the locations for most of these diagrams are inaccurate, as loot found in chests is generally random. Some of these are consistent, however. For example, nearly all of the Superior Potions that are sold by Gremist, according to this list, will consistently be sold by him. The automatic access to Thunderbolt via tutorial in White Orchard is also consistent):

            • Can be found in the Drahim Castle basement.
