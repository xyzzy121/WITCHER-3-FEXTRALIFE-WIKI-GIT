            Steel Weapons
            Silver Swords
            Ranged Weapons
            Bolts

             

            Steel Weapons in the Witcher 3: Wild Hunt are used against specific types of enemies (primarily humans). These weapons vary in damage as they scale with your level. Generally when you pick one up it will be between 3-5 levels below your current level. The bonuses are always the same, but they vary in amount based on the level of the weapon. All Steel Swords have the same damage at the same level.
